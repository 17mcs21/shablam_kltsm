KOTTAYAM

Kottayam is known as the Land of Letters, Latex, and Lakes. And it is not only the first fully literate city in India but also one of immense natural beauty with panoramic views of various water bodies in and around and lush green paddy fields. \nThere is plenty for tourists to see while visiting Kottayam. Erumeli is an important pilgrimage centre for Hindus and Muslims. The annual food festival, which takes place in January, is an exciting festival to experience, and a must-visit for foodies. \nIn July, August and September, Kottayam hosts around 50 different kinds of boat races and these are a lot of fun to watch. Kottayam, with its wondrous natural beauty also makes for a good honeymoon destination.
