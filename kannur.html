KANNUR

Kannur is a beach town located along the northern coast of Kerala in India. It is known for its pristine white beaches that are relatively undiscovered as compared to the coastal cities of southern Kerala. Kannur town headquarters the Kannur district and is known as the land of 'loom and lore' for the handloom industries that operate here and the Theyyam folk ritual that is performed in Kannur. \n\nIt is a sleepy town that has a rich medieval history when it was a famed trading port, identified by Marco Polo as the 'Emporium of Spice Trade.' Because of its history as a thriving trading port, that was once coveted by all the colonial powers. Kannur's location is of strategic importance. It is home to a military cantonment and the Ezhimala Naval Academy, which is the third largest naval academy in the world. If you are seeking some peace and quiet abutting the sea without the paraphernalia of a busy beach town, then Kannur is the perfect destination for you.
